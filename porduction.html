July Developer Newsletter | .live Special Edition

MongoDB.live 2021 was our biggest event yet, built completely by — and for — developers. The most exciting news from the event was the release of MongoDB 5.0 and the arrival of serverless databases in MongoDB Atlas.
.live Keynote Presentation

MongoDB CEO Dev Ittycheria kicked off MongoDB.live with his keynote address, providing an inside look at all the latest updates and product announcements from MongoDB. Joining Dev was Sahir Azam (CPO) and Mark Porter (CTO) to discuss Time Series Collections, Atlas Search, Data Lake, and much more.

MongoDB Atlas Updates

Introducing Atlas serverless instances

Deploy a serverless instance, a new database deployment option in Atlas that automatically and dynamically scales to meet your workload. Simply select a cloud region to get started and pay only for the resources you use.
Read the blog | View the tutorial

Fine-tune relevance with Atlas Search

Understanding the behavior of your users is essential when thinking about search result relevance. With Atlas Search, you can now influence rankings with function scoring and show results for more queries with synonym mappings.
